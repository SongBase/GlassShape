The Glass Shutters program can be configured to suit a variety of hinge geometries by setting appropriate values for edge gaps, hole positions and diameters.  This is done by assigning values to the parameters shown below.

Note that hole positions are shown as the distance from the edge of the glass panel to the centre of the hole.  You should check with your glass supplier that you comply with their requirements for minimum edge distances to holes.


sideGap  = distance between glass and vertical staff beads
endGap  = distance between glass and horizontal staff beads
intGap    = distance between glass panels
